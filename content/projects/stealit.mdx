Paste a URL and extract every visual asset from the page. Pulls out images, GIFs, videos, SVGs, favicons, Open Graph images, and YouTube thumbnails with metadata.

Assets appear in a filterable grid organized by type. Preview any asset with a click, then download individually or grab everything in bulk.

Built with Cheerio for server-side HTML parsing, delivering fast and reliable extraction.
